\# Dataset Requirements



The dataset should contain:



\- Draft Year

\- Draft Pick

\- Player Name

\- Position

\- College

\- Height

\- Weight

\- Age (if available)



Career Statistics:



\- Points

\- Rebounds

\- Assists

\- Games Played

\- Seasons Played



Advanced Metrics (optional):



\- PER

\- Win Shares

\- BPM

\- VORP



Goals:



1\. Identify Draft Steals

2\. Identify Draft Busts

3\. Analyze Position Success

4\. Analyze College Success

5\. Build Draft Intelligence Dashboard

6\. Support Statistical Analysis

7\. Support Predictive Modeling
