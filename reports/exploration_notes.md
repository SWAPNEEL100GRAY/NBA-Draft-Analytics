\## Insight 1: Draft Volume vs Player Quality



Kentucky (58) and Duke (57) produced the highest number of NBA draft picks.



However, Duke players generated higher average career Win Shares (22.22) compared to Kentucky players (19.11).



UConn produced only 31 drafted players but achieved an average Win Share of 29.48, suggesting stronger average career outcomes despite lower draft volume.



Key Observation:

Producing more NBA players does not necessarily mean producing more successful NBA players.
